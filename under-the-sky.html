Under The Sky

5 strangers laid side by side with nothing in between them, but only the vast open sky, to have a facilitated conversation on their personal journeys where mental health are concerned.

Under The Sky is an ode to vulnerability, vastness and openness. It challenges the need for space and proximity to feel safe and close to another person. It pushes us city people to connect with nature and sky. In the moment itself, there is no destination, no place to be. It is just us, the sky, and the people next to us. In this moment, can you find the courage to be vulnerable and true under the sky? To feel yourself deeply and allow yourself to be felt. That there is nobody else you need to be other than yourself- you made up of the entire sum of the good and the bad in you.
